## When to upload documents

Upload documents whenever you receive or generate a file related to a client or debt account:
- Intake paperwork and signed agreements
- Creditor account statements
- Hardship documentation from the client
- Settlement agreement letters
- Proof of payment receipts

## How to upload a document

1. Navigate to the **Client** detail page or the **Debt Account** detail page
2. Scroll to the **Documents** section
3. Click **Upload Document**
4. Select a file from your computer (supported: PDF, JPG, PNG, WebP, DOC, DOCX, TXT; max 4 MB)
5. Select the **Category** (see below)
6. Add optional **Notes** to describe the document
7. Click **Upload**

The file is stored securely in private cloud storage and is only accessible through the DMS.

## Document categories

| Category | Use for |
|----------|---------|
| **Intake** | Enrollment forms, signed agreements, power of attorney |
| **Statement** | Account statements from creditors |
| **Hardship** | Client hardship letters, financial hardship documentation |
| **Settlement Agreement** | Written settlement agreements from creditors |
| **Proof of Payment** | Payment receipts or confirmation letters |
| **Other** | Anything that does not fit the above categories |

## How to download a document

Click the **Download** link next to any document to retrieve the original file. The file downloads with its original filename through the server.

## How to delete a document

Click the **Delete** button on the document row. This permanently removes the file from storage and the database. This action cannot be undone.

## Tips

- Always use the most specific category — this makes documents easier to find during audits or reviews
- Add notes to settlement agreements describing the key terms
- Link documents to the specific debt account when possible, not just the client — this keeps account pages complete

## Common mistakes

- Uploading all documents under "Other" — take 5 seconds to choose the right category
- Uploading files larger than 4 MB — compress large PDFs before uploading
- Forgetting to upload the settlement agreement after a deal is finalized
