## What the Reports page shows

The **Reports** page provides a portfolio-level statistical summary across all clients and accounts. Use it to understand overall program performance, settlement rates, and client status distribution.

## Available statistics

- **Total clients** — count by enrollment status
- **Debt account breakdown** — count and balance by account status
- **Settlement metrics** — total settled accounts and settled amounts
- **Task summary** — open and overdue task counts

## When to use Reports

- Monthly performance reviews
- Identifying portfolio risk (high ratio of charged-off or disputed accounts)
- Checking whether the team's workload is balanced

## Difference from the Dashboard

| | Dashboard | Reports |
|-|-----------|---------|
| **Focus** | Today's to-do list | Portfolio-wide summary |
| **Freshness** | Live / real-time | Snapshot on page load |
| **Best for** | Daily operations | Management review |

## Tips

- Use Reports for management conversations — it gives a clean number summary without operational noise
- Compare Reports data month-over-month to track program improvement
