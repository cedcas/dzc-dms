## What is the Dashboard?

The Dashboard is your daily home base. It gives you an at-a-glance summary of what needs attention right now — follow-ups due today, overdue tasks, account status counts, and recent team activity.

## Dashboard sections

### Stat cards

The four cards at the top show:

| Card | What it means |
|------|--------------|
| **Active Clients** | Clients with status ONBOARDING or ACTIVE |
| **Open Accounts** | Debt accounts with status ACTIVE or IN NEGOTIATION |
| **Follow-ups Today** | Tasks with a due date of today |
| **Overdue Tasks** | Tasks with a due date in the past that are not done |

Use the overdue count as a daily priority signal — if it is high, focus on clearing those first.

### Follow-ups Due Today

Lists tasks due today ordered by time. Each row shows:

- Priority badge (Low / Medium / High / Urgent)
- Task title
- Linked client name
- Assigned team member

This section is your morning checklist. Start here before checking email.

### Overdue Tasks

Lists tasks that are past their due date and still open. These need immediate attention or rescheduling. Click through to the task or client to take action.

### Accounts by Status

Shows a count of debt accounts in each status: Active, In Negotiation, Settled, Charged Off, Disputed, Withdrawn. This gives a quick portfolio health snapshot.

### Recent Activity

Shows the last 8 negotiation activities logged across all accounts — who did what and when. Useful for team leads to see what is happening across the portfolio.

## Tips

- Check the Dashboard first thing in the morning before going to the client list
- If Follow-ups Today shows 0, verify that tasks are being created with due dates when activities are logged
- Overdue Tasks > 5 is a warning sign that tasks are not being completed or rescheduled

## Common mistakes

- Treating the Dashboard as read-only — use it to navigate directly to overdue items
- Missing the Accounts by Status section — it is the fastest way to spot a spike in Charged Off accounts
