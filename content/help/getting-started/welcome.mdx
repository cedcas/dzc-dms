## What is DMS?

The **DebtZero Central Debt Management System (DMS)** is an internal tool designed to help the DZC team manage the full lifecycle of debt resolution. It gives your team a single place to:

- Track client intake and enrollment
- Manage debt accounts for each client
- Log every negotiation activity (calls, emails, letters, settlements)
- Record and manage offers from creditors
- Assign and track follow-up tasks
- Store and organize client documents
- View reports on portfolio performance

## Who uses DMS?

DMS is an internal-only application for DebtZero Central staff. Access is controlled by user roles:

| Role | What you can do |
|------|----------------|
| **Admin** | Everything — including user management |
| **Negotiator** | Clients, accounts, activities, offers, tasks, documents |
| **Intake** | Client intake and basic account entry |
| **Read Only** | View all data, no edits |

## How to sign in

1. Go to the DMS URL provided by your admin
2. Enter your email address and password
3. Click **Sign In**

If you cannot sign in, contact your system administrator to verify your account is active.

## Navigating the app

Use the **left sidebar** to move between the main modules:

- **Dashboard** — Your daily overview: follow-ups, overdue tasks, and recent activity
- **Clients** — All client records
- **Tasks** — Global task list across all clients
- **Creditors** — Creditor profiles
- **Reports** — Portfolio-level statistics

## Common mistakes

- Forgetting to log negotiation activities after a call or email — these are required for accurate follow-up tracking
- Not linking a debt account to the correct creditor — always search for an existing creditor before creating a new one
- Uploading documents without selecting the correct category — use the right category so documents are easy to find later
